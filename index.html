About Me

Engineer. Designer.
Innovator. Automator.

I am a Mechanical Project Engineer who has delivered engineering solutions for some of East Africa's most iconic projects — from the $5 billion EACOP crude oil pipeline to solar energy systems for UNHCR's refugee operations in northern Uganda.
